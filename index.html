Introduction

Hindu literature encompasses a vast body of ancient texts written in Sanskrit and other languages, spanning philosophy, spirituality, epics, hymns, and rituals.

The Vedas

The four Vedas—Rigveda, Samaveda, Yajurveda, and Atharvaveda—form the foundation of Hindu knowledge, containing hymns, rituals, and spiritual insights.
