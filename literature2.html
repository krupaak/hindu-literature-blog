Epics: Ramayana & Mahabharata

The Ramayana, by Valmiki, narrates the story of Lord Rama’s exile and triumph over evil. The Mahabharata, attributed to Vyasa, is the longest epic poem in the world and includes the Bhagavad Gita.

Philosophical Texts

The Upanishads and Bhagavad Gita are revered texts offering deep insights into the nature of self, dharma, karma, and moksha.
